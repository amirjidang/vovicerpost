George Clooney Wedding Tuxedo Predictions: What He Should Wear

August 14, 2024
post

While we get over our disappointment that we’ll be the ones to change George Clooney‘s bachelor ways (well done, Amal Alamuddin), our daydreams of what Clooney, 52, will wear on his wedding to his “ideal match” have just begun.

Here, Us Weekly picks Clooney’s best red carpet styles that he should channel on his big day.

A no-brainer would be the “Classic Clooney,” otherwise known as his go-to notch lapel tuxedo by Giorgio Armani. He’s worn this tux repeatedly on the red carpet in recent years, as at the 2013 BAFTA Los Angeles Britannia Awards and the Gravity premiere at the Venice International Film Festival in 2013.

Otherwise, the Monuments Men hunk could opt for a shawl collar tuxedo like the one he wore to the 2013 BAFTAs.

For a dressy look that is less formal, the Oscar winner may want to consider the traditional black suit with matching tie as at the Gravity premiere in New York City.

Then again, the silver fox may go casual like his look at the Oscars Nominees Luncheon in 2012.
