It was recently reported that Leonardo DiCaprio and Gigi Hadid had reconciled their relationship, but that hasn’t stopped the pair from getting close to new people recently.

Leonardo, 48, was seen getting very close to model Vittoria Ceretti, 25, at the Hï Ibiza nightclub in Ibiza, as captured in photos taken a month ago, which were just published by the Daily Mail. According to the publication, the couple was seen sharing a variety of intimate moments, which included dancing against a wall, laughing, and exchanging kisses until the early hours of the morning.

The actor was wearing his customary black baseball cap and coordinated it with a matching T-shirt for the EDM-themed event. In contrast, Ceretti chose to wear a glittering one-shoulder top and styled her hair in a ponytail.

Vittoria Ceretti Is Married, While Leonardo Was Reportedly Dating Gigi Hadid

Interestingly, this isn’t the first time Leonardo and Vittoria have been romantically linked. They were first connected in August after they were photographed spending time together in Santa Barbara. Images showed the pair appearing to have an intense conversation, while enjoying ice cream and a few beverages.

It appears Vittoria is still legally married. She wed Matteo Milleri in 2020 and there have been no reports of a divorce filing between the couple.

Since the Daily Mail’s photos were taken a month ago, it remains unclear if Leonardo and Vittoria are still romantically involved. But it was reported only weeks ago that Leonardo had reconnected with Gigi Hadid, 28, though they remain non-exclusive.

Leo and Gigi were first linked in September 2020, only to break-up the following February. They appeared to get back together a few weeks later, though they’ve kept their relationship on the down-low in the month since.

The newest report says the pair remain in touch but aren’t exclusively dating. “Leo and Gigi still talk on occasion, and they see each other fairly often because they travel in similar circles of friends,” a source told Us Weekly, adding that Gigi is unwilling to “settle down” with Leo.

However, Gigi Seems To Be Moving On With Someone New

It’s possible that Gigi is unbothered by Leonardo’s romance with Vittoria, given the non-exclusive status of their relationship. But Gigi and Vittoria have developed a friendship as they both work in the modeling industry, so it’s also possible that seeing Leonardo get close to a friend could rub Gigi the wrong way.

Moreover, Gigi may be moved on entirely from Leonardo, as she’s reportedly getting serious with someone new – music produce Cole Bennett. They’ve been spotted together multiple times over the last few weeks.

“They’ve been friends for a few years,” an insider told Us Weekly. “They’ve been playing coy about their status, but they mesh really well together.” The same source added that Gigi is “ready for something more serious" than Leonardo is willing to offer her, and she finds her new romance with Cole to be “refreshing.”

Gigi hasn’t been in a public relationship with 2021, when her on-and-off romance with Zayn Malik came to an end after he allegedly got into an altercation with her mother, Yolanda Hadid. The exes share one daughter.
