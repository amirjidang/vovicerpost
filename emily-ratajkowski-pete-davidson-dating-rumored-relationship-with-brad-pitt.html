The Rumors About Brad Pitt And Emily Ratajkowski

Over the last twenty years, Brad Pitt has been married to two massively successful women, Jennifer Aniston and Angelina Jolie. Unfortunately, both of those pairings that some people considered dream couples came to an end with scandalous divorces.

No matter how Brad Pitt's two marriages ended, he once again becomes one of the most eligible bachelors in the world anytime he becomes a single man.

Since a lot of people consider Brad Pitt to be so desirable, it isn't overly surprising that there have been some rumors about him dating different women. Still, when you look at the recent rumors about Pitt's love life, it is staggering to see how many ladies people once thought he was dating.

Since the year 2017, there have been rumors about Brad Pitt dating Kate Hudson, Sienna Miller, Charlize Theron, Alia Shawkat, and Andra Day among others. In some of those cases, those relationships have been denied they ever dated Pitt but still, it is amazing to see how many rumors about his love life there have been.

Most recently, in late 2022 there were rumors about Brad Pitt and Emily Ratajkowski dating. According to a "Hollywood insider" who spoke to Page Six, Pitt and Ratajkowski wasn't a full-fledged couple yet but they were getting very close.

“People have been speculating about this for a while. Brad is not dating anybody. They [Pitt and Ratajkowski] have been seen a couple times together.”

While the insider was clear that in recent months Brad Pitt has “been seen with other people”, they also said to "stay tuned" for potential news about him and Emily Ratajkowski becoming a couple.

A month before Page Six's report, OK! Magazine quoted an insider who claimed that Pitt asked Ratajkowski out even though she only recently filed for divorce herself.

“He asked her out, and she said yes. She’s always thought Brad was cute, and the way she saw it, what did she have to lose?”

On the other hand, when the public learns that Pete Davidson has gotten involved with the same kind of woman, a lot of people think something like "really, that guy?"

With that in mind, some people may think that it is a no-brainer for Emily Ratajkowski to pick Brad Pitt over Pete Davidson in a theoretical world where they were competing for her. However, things aren't that simple.

When Emily Ratajkowski spoke about Pete Davidson on Late Night with Seth Meyers, her affection for what she knew about him as a person seemed really genuine. With that in mind, if Ratajkowski has opted to be with Brad Pitt solely because of how good-looking he is, Davidson may be a better partner for her.
