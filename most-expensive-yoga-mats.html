Yoga is becoming a common practice in many places. Though it's considered a spiritual practice, it's also a great way to get some exercise. Many people from moms to students to executives enjoy a mindful vinyasa. Breathe in, breathe out, bring your hands to your heart and a smile to your lips. Roll up your luxury mat and have a wheatgrass shot to-go on your way out.

Not all yogis splurge on big-ticket custom yoga gear, but for those that do, there's a long list of places you can shop from. Here are the 10 Most Expensive Yoga Mats Ever.

Manduka Yoga Mat Pro XL - $140

The Manduka Yoga Mat Pro was created in an effort to be more eco-friendly, and safe for consumers. The founder of this company was an architect who began practicing yoga and decided to create a mat that is built to last.

Made with simple and raw materials whenever possible, it's no wonder this mat is beloved by many. Its non-slip surface is said to be great and is well designed. First marketed to famous yoga instructors, including Rodney Yee, this mat brand has sparked lots of interest over the years.

Off-White Logo Yoga Mat - $180

Off-White is a luxury streetwear brand that has taken the world by force. It was founded in 2012 in Milan, Italy, and has since been worn by dazzling celebrities like Rihanna, Kendall Jenner,  Drake, 2 Chainz, and even Beyoncé.

If you do happen to be a yogi who appreciates a cultural phenomenon, this may be the mat made especially for you. It's clear this mat was made to be shown off, in that case, this logo branded yoga mat is sure to set you apart in your power yoga class.

Jade Yoga XW Fusion Mat - $179.95

Jade Yoga's origin is quite interesting. It was first a rug company that also created rubber padding. As BKS Iyengar demonstrated some postures in the US, he was saved from slipping when the rug was removed and he practiced on the pad.

Jade Yoga has since perfected a mat that is non-toxic, and non-slip. The mat is extra-wide so yogis don't need to scoot around and reposition when practicing tricky postures. The mat is also 5/16" thick and is great for the extra support for knees and inversion postures.

Holistic Silk Luxurious Yoga Mat - $230

Perhaps you are an eco-friendly yogi who prefers a more soothing or restorative practice like Yin Yoga or Yoga Nidra. If so, the Holistic Silk Luxurious Mat may be the perfect mat for you. This company was founded in 1999 and makes very well crafted sustainable handmade mats.

The yoga mat is handmade from vegan materials including synthetic leather and soft fleece. It is also stuffed with lavender flowers, which can make your practice nicely scented. The mats are lightweight, portable and an additional bonus is that they are washable.

Maati Maati Yoga Mat - $250

If you're a sustainable yogi, check out the Maati Maati yoga mat. It's made from natural cork from Portugal. This mat is eco-friendly and sustainable as it comes from the local cork oak tree. These mats are also made locally in Portugal, and the cork is harvested by farmers who employ ancient techniques.

To top it off 10% of proceeds from this cushiony mat are donated to protecting flora and fauna in Portugal. The material makes it easy to clean, so it's also recommended for a sweaty vinyasa yoga session.

Saint Laurent No Ka'oi Yoga Mat - $360

This mat is a stylish crossover by Saint Laurent Paris in collaboration with No Ka'oi. If you're a yogi that loves a brand name, this one may be for you. No Ka'oi means "the best" in Hawaiian, though it was founded in Bologna, Italy in 2014.

This brand has an emphasis on construction and playful colors, activewear, and streetwear, and has even been seen on celebrities like Kandi Burruss. If the black mat doesn't suit you, there is also a leopard print version you can take home for €345 (around $360).

Liforme x Burberry - $420

If you happen to be in the market for a well-made mat, with a design that is both sleek and utilitarian, look no further. Liforme's recent collaboration with Burberry is a simple, sturdy mat equipped with the AlignForMe alignment guides that are on all of Liforme's yoga mats.

This mat is non-slip and extra comfy. Liforme mats were developed over a period of 5 years by an entertainment lawyer who also practices yoga. They're designed for both comfort and utility, so users can really find their body in the pose.

Ermenegildo Zegna - $500

The illustrious Ermenegildo Zegna brand was born in Trivero, Northern Italy in 1910. The company has since grown massively in the public eye and is now considered one of the most influential brands in luxury men's attire. Ermenegildo Zegna's menswear can be seen all across the world sported by their newest brand campaign model, Academy Award winner, Mahersala Ali.

And just in case you're wondering, it can also be seen on many celebrity actors like Adam Driver, Robert Downey Junior, and Tom Cruise.

Baller Yoga LXXX Mat - $1,500

With good sportsmanship in mind, the Baller yoga mat was created to combine yoga and football. It's crafted from the same leather as the NFL uses to equip its ballers. The founder is a traveler, inventor, and yogi who credits long meditation at a retreat to his newest innovation.

The mat packs a good smell since it's made locally in the United States and has a non-slip grip. It's said to be perfect for any baller yogi that is looking for a genuine leather mat that will help them tackle the poses.

Chakracarma - $15,000 to $100,000

We have come to the créme de la creme, the world's most expensive yoga mat made by Chakracarma. It is fully customizable and features finely tanned leather and precious adornments you can choose from.

The mat has a starting price of $15,000 but can actually cost up to $100,000 depending on which precious gemstones are calling your name. The Chakracarma mat can feature diamond, ruby, emerald, sapphire, amber, opal or carnelian stones which carry a specific vibration that can open up your chakras or affect your energetic body.
