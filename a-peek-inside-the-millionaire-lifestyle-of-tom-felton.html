Tom Felton hails from Surrey, England, and has steadily grown his career in music and movies. He first began working in commercials and then played minor characters in British films before landing the role of Draco Malfoy in Harry Potter, which is a eight-movie series. Felton also starred in drama and action television shows and movies to improve his acting portfolio. The actor is known to splash his earnings on buying luxury cars, real estate. Tom also spends millions on pursuing hobbies such as golf and photography.

A majority of Felton’s earnings have been spent in purchasing the most luxurious cars in the world and being an avid photographer, he also collects vintage cameras to capture special moments. From real estate to charity, here is a look at the millionaire lifestyle of Tom Felton.

Real Estate

Tom has spent moderately on real estate and lives in a small town in England called Epsom. The actor also owned a Hollywood Hills mansion in 2014 for $1.275 million. The 1,732 sq. ft. home features two bedrooms and bathrooms with a guest house, a separate kitchen and a sleeping area, as noted by Forbes. The living room is completed with floor-to-ceiling bookshelves, while the kitchen has butcher-block countertops with direct access to the backyard. Felton had listed the property for sale for $1.449 million.

Golf Matches

Felton has always been an enthusiastic golfer and spends time at the links with his friends. As the star lives in LA, he is usually spotted visiting the Mountain Gate Country Club. More recently, Felton took part in a celebrity special Golf tournament in Wisconsin where he was representing England however sadly he had to withdraw his name from the competition due to a medical emergency.

Car Collection

A gearhead at heart, Felton owns a Lamborghini Urus in black, and he shared a snapshot of his it parked outside the Warner Bros. Studios lot while shooting the Harry Potter reunion. As mentioned by Auto Evolution, he also owns a Lamborghini Aventador S, which was first introduced in 2017 and is equipped with a powerful 6.5-liter V12 that delivers 730 HP with 509 lb-ft of torque. He also has a $287,400 Lamborghini Huracán EVO Spyder in his garage.

Harry Potter Merchandise

There is not a bigger Potterhead than Tom Felton himself. Along with starring in the movie series, he is also the biggest supporter of its community online and likes to interact with fans. As a Slytherin in the movie, Felton is often spotted wearing the Harry Potter's Slytherin merchandise on his social media accounts and posing with his dog Willow. Surprisingly, Tom was sorted into Gryffindor in real life, which he finds hard to believe.

Vacations

The millionaire throws a good chunk of his fortune on vacations with his friends and loved ones. He was often spotted hitting the beach with his then-girlfriend Jade Olivia in popular locales such as Miami, Florida. In 2019, Felton sent fans into a frenzy when he reunited with his former co-star Emma Watson and they vacationed together in South Africa, where the actor taught Watson how to play the guitar, as stated by Mirror.

Music & Photography

As a talented singer, Tom has released some music over the years, showing off his golden pipes as he was trained in the church choir from the age of seven. Another hobby on the list is his love for photography. Felton is often spotted with a camera, capturing essential moments around his family and landscape images. He owns a vintage Leica camera that costs upwards of $5,799.

Virtual Parties

Felton has hosted a series of virtual parties over the years. Recently, he celebrated the 19th Anniversary of Harry Potter by throwing a virtual gathering for fans around the world. He played music, shared his experience about the world of Hogwarts, and even gave details of his audition process before he landed the role. The party had surprise appearances from several cast members, including Daniel Radcliffe and Rupert Grint.

Philanthropy

A man with a big heart, he is known to conduct various charity events to raise money. In 2007, at the peak of his career, the actor visited the Children’s Hospital in Denver, Colorado, for Philanthropy. According to Celebrity Net Worth, in 2011, he also became a part of the Red Nose Day telethon in the UK on the BBC channel to raise money and recently, Tom took part in a golf tournament that helped raise money for various causes.

From adding cars to his garage to helping various causes that raise awareness about the pressing problems in the world, Tom Felton's millionaire lifestyle is commendable. The actor last starred in the drama Save The Cinema in 2022.
