Patti Harrison

Patti Harrison as plays as Allison who is the social media manager of Loretta. American comedian, actress, and writer Patti Harrison has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million. She is mostly known for her roles in numerous comedy projects like the film Together Together and the series Shrill and I Think You Should Leave along with Tim Robinson. Her portrayal for the movie Together Together has earned her an Independent Spirit Award for Best Female Lead nomination.

Da'Vine Joy Randolph plays as Beth Hatten who is the publicist of Loretta. American actress and singer Da'Vine Joy Randolph has an estimated net worth of $2 million. She rose to fame after she played a Oda Mae Brown in the Broadway production of the musical Ghost: Musical in 2012. She was nominated for Tony Award's Best Performance by a Featured Actress in a musical for her portrayal as Oda Mae. Her career as an actress and singer has earned her millions.

Bowen Yang

Bowen Yang plays as Ray the Moderator in the movie. Australian-born American actor, comedian, podcaster, and writer Bowen Yang has an estimated net worth of $3 million. He has soared high into the comedy stratosphere at an extremely exponential speed the last few years. He was originally hired as part of the staff writers for Saturday Night Live however he ended up being part of the team as Saturday Night Live cast member. He has brought in excitement on the show since he was the first Chinese-American cast member and a proud member of the LGBTQ+ community.

Oscar Nuñez

Oscar Nuñez plays as Oscar who is an eccentric cargo plane pilot. Cuban-American actor, comedian, and writer Oscar Nuñez has an estimated net worth of $5 million. He is best known for his role in the widely successful NBC TV series, The Office, playing as the Dunder Mifflin accountant Oscar Martinez. He was initially a member of The Groundlings however he later became a regular cast member on the series as he was loved by the audience.

Channing Tatum

Channing Tatum plays as Alan Caprison / Dash McMahon who is the dimwitted cover model for the novel written by Loretta called The Lost City of D. American actor, dancer, producer, and model Channing Tatum has an estimated net worth of $80 million. He is among the high paying actors in Hollywood and was even named as the highest paid actor in the world during his peak. This was all thanks to his highly lucrative deal for the movie Magic Mike which paid sky high amount of $21 million for the Magic Mike XXL alone.

Daniel Radcliffe

Daniel Radcliffe plays as Abigail Fairfax who is the eccentric billionaire and international criminal who later one kidnapped Loretta just so he can find the lost city, on which her novel is based. British actor Daniel Radcliffe has an estimated net worth of $110 million. He is known to be among the wealthiest celebrities in United Kingdom and best known for his role in the extensively successful Harry Potter film franchise.

Sandra Bullock

Sandra Bullock plays as the frustrated and depressed best selling romance novelist Loretta Sage / Angela in the film. The Virginia-born actress and producer currently has an estimated net worth of $250 million. Upon moving to Los Angeles, she has maintained a steady roster of minor roles before landing a major role in 1993's Demolition Man with Sylvester Stallone and Wesley Snipes. The year after, she was casts as the main lead for the film Speed co-starring with Keanu Reeves and since then, she has been tagged as America’s sweetheart. The Lost City is her latest film offering to her fans.

Brad Pitt

Brad Pitt as plays as Jack Trainer who is a Navy Seal-turned-CIA agent. American film actor and producer Brad Pitt has an estimated net worth of $300 million. The award winning actor is among the most famous and highest paid entertainers in the world and is consistently earning at least $20 million on every major motion picture releases he was featured on. He has been nominated numerous times for Academy Awards and won on 2014 with Academy Award as a Producer.
