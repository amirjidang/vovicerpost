For decades, this iconic soap opera has captivated audiences with its compelling storylines and memorable characters. From cunning villains to star-crossed lovers, the cast of "General Hospital" has become a staple of American television. But have you ever wondered who among them has accumulated the most wealth?

Let’s take a closer look at the current cast of "General Hospital" and rank them by their net worth. Discover the fortunes of these beloved actors and actresses who have brought life to Port Charles, and find out who reigns supreme in terms of financial success. So, grab a seat and get ready to dive into the fascinating world of wealth and fame in the realm of "General Hospital."

Cynthia Watros - $1.5 Million

In 2019, after Michelle Stafford's departure from The Young and the Restless, Cynthia Watros was chosen to portray Nina Reeves in General Hospital. Although she had to step into some big shoes, Watros proved to be an exceptional actress with her unique talent, and the audience quickly embraced her. Her net worth has been steadily growing since her debut in the series New York Undercover and is now estimated to be around $1.5 million. Watros has also made her mark in the industry with notable roles in Another World, Guiding Light, Titus, and Lost.

Tanisha Harper - $2 Million

Tanisha Harper is a fresh face on the popular soap opera General Hospital. While many actors have graced the show for decades, Harper is just beginning her career in the soap opera industry. In 2022, she was cast as Jordan Ashford, taking over the role of actress Brianna Nicole Henry, who departed the show due to her pregnancy after portraying the character for three years. Harper has already made her mark, amassing a reported net worth of $2 million through her work in series like Desire and Dollface, among others.

Maurice Benard - $2 Million

After joining the cast of General Hospital in 1993, Maurice Benard has truly made the show his home. Portraying the complex character of Sonny Corinthos, a crime lord grappling with mental health issues, Benard has captivated audiences with his portrayal. While General Hospital remains one of Benard's most popular projects, he has also made significant strides in other areas, amassing a net worth of $2 million.

Nancy Lee Grahn - $3 Million

Nancy Lee Grahn is currently valued at $3 million. Many years have passed since Nancy Lee Grahn joined General Hospital, and it does not appear that she has any plans to leave any time soon. In 2021, she stated that there was "no end in sight" and that they would have to "drag me out kicking and screaming." She has increased her net worth through her many years on the show and in other notable films and television programs. She has appeared in Models Inc., Murder One, Melrose Place, and 7th Heaven, as well as the films Kids Don't Tell and Perry Mason: The Case of the Glass Coffin, among others.

Kelly Monaco - $4 Million

Kelly Monaco has been portraying Sam McCall on General Hospital since 2003. She has amassed a net worth of $4 million through her profession as an actress and a model. While the show was a significant part of her career, she has accomplished many remarkable accomplishments in her life. Her performance in Port Charles garnered her an Emmy nomination. In addition to appearing on Dancing with the Stars and Baywatch, she has also modeled for Playboy.

Donnell Turner - $5 Million

Donell Turner joined General Hospital's ensemble in 2015. Turner has accumulated a net worth of $5 million despite the fact that he has not been a part of the serial opera for nearly as long as other cast members. Before landing the role of Curtis Ashford on General Hospital, Turner appeared on numerous prominent television programs as a guest star. He appeared in four episodes of Days of Our Lives and two episodes of Young and the Restless.

Kirsten Storms - $6 Million

Last year marked the 15th anniversary of Kirsten Storms' appearance on the program. She was selected in the role of Maxie Jones, which had been played for eleven years by Robyn Richards. She resumed her work where she left off, with only a brief interruption due to a health emergency, and has been doing an outstanding job for the past 16 years. Her current net worth is $6 million.

Finola Hughes - $8 Million

General Hospital would be nearly unimaginable without Finola Hughes. She was cast as Anna Devane in 1985, and although she has portrayed other characters over the years, that is unquestionably her most recognizable role. Anna Devane is a highly accomplished spy who faces stiff competition from the new wife of her ex-husband. Finola has been an actress for several decades and has garnered an impressive $8 million fortune as a result of her work on this show, All My Children, and numerous other projects.

Laura Wright - $10 Million

Laura Wright has been on the program since 2005 and has amassed a $10 million fortune as a result of her time there and her colorful and extensive career. She is arguably best known for her portrayal of Carly Corinthos, a woman who viewers love and loathe with equal fervor. In 2011, her talent garnered her the Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series Daytime Emmy Award. She may also be recognized for her roles in Loving, Guiding Light, and The City.

Genie Francis - $25 Million

Genie Francis holds the distinction of being General Hospital's second-longest-serving cast member, directly behind Leslie Charleson. Since she entered the show in 1977, Genie has portrayed Laura Spencer for over four decades. She has also participated in All My Children, Bare Essence, North and South, as well as numerous other productions. She is at the summit of this list, having accumulated a net worth of $25 million.
