TNA IMPACT Spoilers From Orlando (Taped On 1/20)

August 11, 2024
post

On January 20, TNA held the second night of their TNA IMPACT television tapings from Osceola Heritage Park in Orlando, Florida.

The spoilers, courtesy of the Wrestling Observer via PWInsider, are as follows:

  • Big Kon def. Ori Gold & Richard Adonis in a handicap match
    • Kon kept attacking his opponents after the match until PCO made the save.
  • Tasha Steelz def. Xia Brookside
  • Frankie Kazarian def. Jake Something
  • Jordynne Grace def. Savannah Evans
    • After the match, Gisele Shaw attacked Grace and Evans.
  • ABC (Ace Austin & Chris Bey) def. The Grizzled Young Veterans (Zack Gibson & James Drake) in the second match of their best-of-three series
  • TNA Digital Media Champion Crazzy Steve def. Rhino
  • Joe Hendry def. Deaner
  • Joe Hendry had a new music video that was a parody of AJ Styles’ TNA theme.
  • Dani Luna def. Killer Kelly
    • Kelly & Masha Slamovich attacked Luna and Jody Threat after the bell. Decay (Havok & Rosemary) made the save. 
  • Chris Sabin def. Jason Hotch
  • John Skyler cut a promo and talked about facing Chris Sabin for the X-Division title at TNA Sacrifice.
  • Simon Gotch def. Jack Price
    • After the match, Gotch and Josh Alexander had a face-to-face confrontation.
  • Eric Young def. Big Damo
    • Before the match, Frankie Kazarian told Young that he had to beat someone from his past before they faced each other.
  • Ash By Elegance def. Savannah Thorne
  • The System (Moose, Brian Myers & Eddie Edwards) defeated KUSHIDA, Alex Shelley & Kevin Knight
