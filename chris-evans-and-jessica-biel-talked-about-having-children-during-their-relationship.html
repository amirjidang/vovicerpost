Some celebrity couples are able to maintain private relationships, staying under the radar for a while and possibly dealing with some rumors before they finally admit publicly to being an official couple.

Chris Evans and Jessica Biel were able to fly under the radar with their own relationship, which began in 2001.

The couple made a few appearances on the red carpet together. They also spoke on occasion about their relationship.

According to ScreenRant, the two were able to have a low-key relationship mainly due to the "timing" in each of their careers. "Evans and Biel managed to slip their relationship of five years under the radar largely thanks to timing."

The article continued, "Biel was still working on overcoming her squeaky clean 7th Heaven days and Evans was still years away from taking up Captain America's shield."

Jessica Biel And Chris Evans Even Discussed Marriage And Having Children

Many celebrities couples can be spotted in public, even if they keep their relationships quiet, or their relationships are more low-key than others. Some celebrities even go on to get married and have children.

While Chris Evans and Jessica Biel were together, the couple discussed both possibilities before their relationship ended for unknown reasons.

According to Cosmopolitan, the couple was spotted being romantic with one another on the red carpet in December 2001. Describing a picture of the couple, the article commented, "The couple is lovey-dovey as they walk the red carpet at the second annual My VH1 Music Awards."

In an October 2005 article with Cosmopolitan, the Candy star discussed her relationship with Evans. She also revealed the two had discussed children and marriage.

"When I turned 21, I actually awoke to find my bed covered in rose petals. In the middle of the night, he had pulled apart at least 24 roses in all different colors and sprinkled the petals everywhere. He's definitely a keeper."
