How Much Did Kim Kardashian's Three Wedding Dresses Cost?

August 24, 2024
post

Kim Kardashian's wedding day looks cost a pretty penny!

A source tells Us Weekly that the estimated total costs of Kim's three dresses, Kris Jenner's dress, Kim's four bridesmaids' dresses and Kris Humphries' mother's dress was somewhere between $135,000 and $150,000.

While the exact cost of each Vera Wang gown is not being disclosed, a source estimates the ivory dress she wore down the aisle cost around $25,000. "[The cost] all depends on the work, the silhouette, the details and the time involved," said the source.

Kris Humphries' tuxedos (he had two) didn't come cheap either. The Zegna suits were made to measure and cost an estimated $3,000 to $4,000 each. A source told Us that Bunim-Murray, who is producing Kim's Fairytale Wedding: A Kardashian Event (set to air in October on E!) picked up the bill for Humphries' tuxes, his groomsmen's and a made to measure suit for Mason Disick, who served as ring bearer.
