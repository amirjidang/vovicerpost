The Cast Of 'Selling Tampa' Ranked By Net Worth

August 18, 2024
post

Netflix's Selling Tampa is yet another insight into the glamorous lives of the rich and classy. The show follows the lifestyles of some high-profile agents from Tampa Bay who close multimillion-dollar real estate listings before breakfast, so much so that it has become everyday life for them.

Selling Tampa is a spin-off from a previously aired show called Selling Sunset, and fans who followed the latter can testify that real estate sale is a pretty lucrative line of work. While their clients have been known to be extremely wealthy, the realtors aren't doing bad for themselves at all. If they earn millions in commission from closing one transaction, we can only wonder how much money they actually have. Here are the richest cast members of the show.

8 Sharelle Rosado - $6 Million

We can't say it's particularly surprising that Sharella Rosado, the CEO of Allure Realty is the wealthiest of the bunch. She's been in the real estate business for a while and launched her company in 2019. Currently, Allure Realty is globally recognized and stands as one of America’s top brokerages. Rosado is presently estimated to have a net worth of $6 million, and just like the characters on the sister show, Selling Sunset, the drama with these rich folks never ends.

7 Rena Frazier - $2 Million

When the CEO has a net worth of close to eight figures, it can't be much of a surprise that her number two is closely behind. Of all the agents in Allure Realty, under Rosado’s supervision, Frazier is the most monied. Even though she has been a part of the company since its early days, she had a pretty outstanding career before it. She holds a law degree and practiced for over seven years before Allure, a time within which she was made a partner at the law firm. Joined with the money she made from her previous line of work, Frazier is currently estimated to have a net worth of $2 million.

6 Juawana Colbert - $1 Million

One of the many qualities that have kept Sharelle Rosado at the top of the food chain in her line of work is her ability to spot the best of the best and bring them in. Juawana Colbert is one of the high achievers recruited by Rosado. Before becoming a part of her company, and by extension the show, she worked in finance. According to information on her LinkedIn profile, she has held several executive positions in the past, including project manager, Vice President of a development council, and business development manager at different organizations. So far, she has worked at Allure for close to three years and is estimated to be worth $1 million.

5 Tennille Moore - $1 Million

Tennille Moore is one of those business-minded women who are actively involved in a variety of fields. While she currently works at Allure Realty, she also holds a certificate proving her a certified credit repair specialist and has her own company in that line of business. Additionally, she also works as a Legislative Assistant at the Florida House of Representatives. All of this, while also closing real estate sales, it's no surprise she has an estimated net worth pegged at $1 million.

4 Karla Giorgio - $800,000

Karla Giorgio's strong point is her professional experience, as she has been in the real estate industry for quite some time now. After obtaining her degree in business from Valencia University, she started to gain major recognition while she climbed up the corporate ladder, and landing a couple of high-profile jobs at different brokerages in 2015, just put the icing on the cake. After a few years in the field, she moved on to Allure Realty where she’s currently living the big-time real estate agent dream. Presently, her net worth is estimated to be $800 thousand.

3 Anne-Sophie Petit-Frere - $700,000

Anne-Sophie Petit-Frere is a graduate of the University of South Florida with a Bachelor’s degree in accounting. Shortly after school, she began her career and things went from good to better for her in the marketing industry. Later the 27-year-old transitioned into real estate, leading her to become part of the Allure team in 2019. Now, she's all about that glamourous life, and with a $700 thousand net worth, she can definitely afford it.

2 Colony Reeves - $600,000

Reeves is also an alumnus of the University of South Florida and according to her LinkedIn profile, her career at Allure began in October 2019. Before that time, she had worked at a different brokerage company for two years. According to the star, she also worked as a preschool teacher early in her life, but after a little time, she quit and got into real estate based on advice from her dad. Now, that career change has paid off in huge checks as she currently sits on an estimated net worth of $600 thousand.

1 Alexis Williams - $400,000

Alexis Williams is another exceptional creative on the team. She joined the company in 2019 and has worked for two years. In that short service time, she has proven without a doubt that she is the woman for the job when it comes to real estate sales. While this was her first time in the real estate world, she has been able to close deals north of a million dollars. Apart from that, she has also occasionally wowed the team with her interior decoration skills, thus making most of her clients keep her around for other deals. Williams has an estimated net worth of $400 thousand.
