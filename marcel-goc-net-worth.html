Marcel Goc is a German professional ice hockey player who has an estimated net worth of $7 million.

Marcel Goc began his career in 1999 when he played for Deutche Eishockey Liga's Schwenningen Wild Wings. After three seasons with the team, he transferred to Adler Mannheim in 2001.

During the 2001 NHL Entry Draft, he was selected by the San Jose Sharks as the first round, 20th overall pick. Before he started for the Sharks, he appeared to play for the Cleveland Barons during the 2003-2004 season.

He joined the Sharks in the 2004 playoffs. After his stint with the team, Goc signed a one-year, two-way contract for the Nashville Predators in 2009. When he became a free agent two years later, he played for the Florida Panthers. He moved to Pittsburgh Penguins in 2014 for a 3rd and 5th round pick.

Marcel Goc was born on August 23, 1983 in Calw, West Germany. He is married to Suzanne Goc.
