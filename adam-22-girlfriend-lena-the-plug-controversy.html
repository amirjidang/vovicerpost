Who Is Lena The Plug?

Lena, whose real name is Lena Nersesian, has not taken what many perceive to be the conventional way of reaching the top. Nevertheless, she has found great success within a short period. Over time, she has found a way to appeal to the desires of her fans – and that has been the secret behind her escalating brand.

But becoming an internet sensation was tricky ground, as Lena was raised by very strict Christian parents. She graduated from the University of California and got a degree in Psychology. The influencer entered grad school, but was not able to finish due to financial problems. So, she started working at a social media startup – where her co-workers began to give her a nickname “Lena The Plug.”

Aware that the job was not a great for her, she started working as a Snapchat model and gradually her fans increased. Now, she has over a million followers on YouTube and earns a handful of money – just as how other personalities became famous because of this platform.

In an interview with Forbes, she shared, “I only got into this business because so many followers kept asking me if I had premium content. Now I use my social media platforms as marketing tools for my business. And as a way to meet the other creators to work with. I owe everything to the internet and these platforms.”
