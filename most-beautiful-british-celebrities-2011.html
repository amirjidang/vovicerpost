TheRichest.org Top 10 Most Beautiful British Actresses for 2011:

This Classification was done by TheRichest.org feel free to disagree,leave a comment…

1-Kate Beckinsale

37 year old english actress. She first gained notice, while a student at Oxford University, for making her debut in the film adaptation of Shakespeare's Much Ado About Nothing.

2-Keira Knightley

25 year old English film actress and model.

3-Emma Watson

20 year old english actress and model who rose to prominence playing Hermione Granger, one of three starring roles in the Harry Potter film series.

4-Rachel Weisz

40 year old english film,theatre actress and fashion model.

5-Freema Agyeman

31 year old British actress of Ghanaian and Iranian descent who is best known for playing Martha Jones, former companion of the Tenth Doctor in the BBC science fiction television series Doctor Who, and its spin-off series Torchwood.

6-Gemma Arterton

25 year old english actress.

7-Thandie Newton

38 year old English actress,she's more popular in U.S for her numerous American films.

8-Billie Piper

28 year old english singer and actress.

9-Marsha Thomason

35 year old english actress, best  known in the U.S  for playing Nessa Holt in the first two seasons of the NBC series Las Vegas, for her recurring role on ABC's Lost as Naomi Dorrit, and for playing FBI agent Diana Barrigan on USA Network's White Collar.

10-Catherine Zeta-Jones

41 year old Welsh actress married to Michael Douglas.
