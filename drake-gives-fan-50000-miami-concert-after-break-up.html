Drake Gifts Heartbroken Fan $50,000 At Miami Concert After Break-Up

August 05, 2024
post

Highlights

  • Drake stops his performance to acknowledge a fan who had just been broken up with before the concert.
  • The rapper reads a sign from the fan explaining the situation, and expresses disbelief that the ex-girlfriend didn't come to the show.
  • Drake surprises the fan by giving him $50,000 as a special gift, saying he can flex on his ex-girlfriend.

Drake made one concert goer’s night very special during his most recent performance in Miami, Florida at Kaseya Center last week.

While on stage, there was a sign that caught the rapper’s attention, which made him stop the performance to point it out. The sign explained that the fan had just been broken up with before the concert, and was originally supposed to attend with his girlfriend.

"I like that sign," Drake told the audience. "That’s a good sign to pick tonight. Usually I’ll do something nice for a lady. I’ll do something nice, I’ll give away a bag or some s--- like that."

"But my bro right here, he said, 'I spent all my savings buying tickets for me and my ex, but Honestly, Nevermind, it's really Her Loss,'" Drake said while reading the text on the sign, which referred to one of his most famous albums. "So, she ain't come with you tonight? She ain't come with you tonight — to the Drake show? What the f--- is wrong with her?" Drake continued. "You in here just icy with the gold chain and the sunglasses on?"

As Drake continued hyping the fan up, other people in the audience began booing the ex-girlfriend, even saying “F*** that b***h.”” However, Drake wasn’t about to stand for the derogatory remarks, as he said, "Alright, that's a little aggressive."

The rapper apparently had a better idea – that’s when he told the fan he was giving him an extra special gift, specifically $50,000.

"You know what? She’s gonna feel real f---ed up ’cause I’ma give you 50 bands, so you gon’ flex on her tonight," he revealed to the cheering crowd. "That’s how we doing it tonight, big dog! It’s your night tonight! And I won’t say it how y’all said it, but f--- that young lady."

Footage of the encounter has gone viral on social media, with many fans hoping they can secure a payout during Drake’s next concert. It’s expected but unconfirmed that Drake’s team arranged for the fan to receive his $50,000 prize following the concert.

This isn’t the only surprise Drake has made at a recent performance. Last month, the entertainer stunned fans by revealing he purchased a home in Houston, Texas, sparking rumors he may be relocating to lead a more low-key lifestyle. Drake has an array of properties in Toronto and California.
