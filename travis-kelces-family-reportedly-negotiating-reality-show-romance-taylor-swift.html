While Taylor Swift’s past romance with Joe Alwyn was kept notoriously private, the same can't be said for her ongoing relationship with Travis Kelce. Not only have the couple put their romance front and center, but Travis seems to be using it to boost his career, and that may include an upcoming foray into reality television.

According to a report by Radar Online, multiple networks are considering giving Travis and his brother Jason Kelce their very own reality show, which would presumably focus on their personal lives as NFL stars.

Travis And Jason Kelce Could Get Their Own Reality Show

In addition to their NFL careers (though Jason announced his retirement earlier this year), the brothers host their New Heights podcast.

"Producers are sniffing around the entire Kelce family, especially Jason and his wife Kylie," a source claimed. "The idea is to make them a more relatable version of the Kardashians."

They added, "The level of interest in the Kelces since Travis started dating Taylor has not gone unnoticed in Hollywood, but it's also very clear that they're super thirsty for attention."

The same source claimed that Travis and Jason are both “embracing every moneymaking opportunity that comes along and they have no problem oversharing, so a reality show seems to be a no-brainer,” which seems to echo reports that Travis’ net worth has substantially increased thanks to his relationship with Taylor, offering new paid promotion opportunities and an increase in his jersey sales, amongst other things.

How Travis Kelce Handles The Pressures That Come With Dating Taylor Swift

Travis Kelce appears to be well-prepared to handle the pressures that come with dating a superstar like Taylor Swift.

While a reality show could bring the Kelce family more fame, it could also increase their security risks. Travis has already admitted to having to sell his Kansas mansion after fans discovered his address. He later invested in a newer property with enhanced security features.

A Reality Show Could Risk The Kelce Family’s Safety

Jason Kelce has spoken about the impact their family’s newfound fame has brought, admitting not all has been positive.

"It's certainly been weird, the level that it is now," Jason told NBC Sports. "On one hand, I'm happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he's excited about, that he is genuine about. But there's another end of it where it's like, ‘Man, this is a lot.'"

Describing the fame as "another level of stardom that typically football players don't deal with, Jason said he’s nevertheless happy for Travis and Taylor.

"So, on one hand, really, really happy for my brother and where he's at in his current situation with Taylor," he continued. "But on the other hand, there's some alarms sometimes with how over-in-pursuit people can be."

Jason added that fans shouldn’t worry the fame is becoming overwhelming for Travis, as he says his younger brother can take it. "Overall, he can deal with some of this," Jason explained. "As long as it's not becoming a threat to his safety and things like that."
