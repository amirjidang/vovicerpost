Popular American kids' show Barney and Friends was created by Sheryl Leach. The show which targeted children aged 2-7 years conveyed important values through songs and dance routines with a friendly approach. Barney opened children's eyes to the joys of learning and discovery, the wonders of make-believe, and the beauty of unconditional love. With its educative yet fun approach, Barney caught the attention of several children around the world. But even more interesting about the show is the long list of actors who got to play Barney.
