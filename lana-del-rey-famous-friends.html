Lana Del Rey And 10 Of Her Famous Friends

One of this generation's most incredible and talented singers is Lana Del Rey and she releases songs that fit into the dream pop and rock music genres. Listening to her songs feels almost magical because of the way her voice and lyrics flow together. Lana Del Rey is known for posting photos on social media with a lot of her non-famous friends. She posts selfies with her best friends all the time and she usually always has a smile on her face when she is with them.

Seeing Lana Del Rey with famous friends is rarer. Continue reading to find out who Lana Del Rey's most famous friends are.

10 Billie Eilish

Lana Del Rey and Billie Eilish are friends who both sing music in a similar genre with a similar sound. They are vastly different, but still very relatable and comparable. Both of these talented singers have spoken out positively about each other in the media, proving to the world that they are friends.

Hopefully, one day Lana Del Rey and Billie Eilish will release a song together because their voices would sound heavenly together.

9 Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner is one of Lana Del Rey's friends. Lana Del Rey was close to the Kardashian/Jenner clan during the time that Kanye West and Kim Kardashian were just getting married and she performed at their wedding. She was rumored to have received $2.8 million dollars to perform three songs, but she denies this and said she performed for free.

It is believable that she wouldn't have charged them to perform since she considers them to be good friends.

8 A$AP Rocky

Lana Del Rey and A$AP Rocky have collaborated together for a couple of awesome songs. They are good friends in real life, which is probably why their collabs came out so great. The first song they have together is A$AP Rocky's song called "Ridin'," from 2012.

The other two songs belong to Lana Del Rey and they feature rap verses from A$AP Rocky. Those songs are "Groupie Love" and "Summer Bummer" and they have more of a hip hop vibe.

7 Taylor Swift

Lana Del Rey and Taylor Swift have one of those friendships that goes beyond the level of being acquaintances. They have songs that have been mashed-up by fans, proving that their music has a similar vibe. Taylor Swift has a song called "Wildest Dream" that sounds like Lana Del Rey's song "Without You."

Taylor Swift started off as a country singer and moved into the world of pop music. Lana Del Rey has always been more in the dream pop and rock genres, but these two still connect, regardless.

6 The Weeknd

Lana Del Rey and The Weeknd have a cool friendship. Along with their close friendship, they also have a few song collaborations together. Pictured here, they are posing for their song, "Lust for Life." This song is all about being young, free, and experiencing romance in Hollywood.

There is something very magical about Lana Del Rey’s music and the fact that she paired up with The Weeknd to create such an amazing song shows how good of a friendship these two have.

5 Katy Perry

Katy Perry and Lana Del Rey are two incredible singers who definitely need to come up collaborate at some point. They have vastly different vocal styles, but that doesn’t matter at all. They are both so beloved by so many people that if they ever put out a song together, it would be great.

Katy Perry has a louder and more colorful persona, while Lana Del Rey has a quieter, softer, and more elegant image, but at the end of the day, they both have beautiful voices and the friendship between them is wholesome.

4 Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa and Lana Del Rey are two singers who also have a great friendship between them. Dua Lipa is someone who rose to fame in 2015. She hasn’t been in the spotlight for that long, but she has made a big name for herself.

On the other hand, Lana Del Rey has been around for over a decade. Regardless of who got famous first or who has been famous longer, both singers are connected as friends.

3 Jared Leto

Lana Del Rey and Jared Leto used to be in a relationship, and even though they aren’t dating anymore, they are still cool with each other. They don’t have anything negative to say about each other in the media which is a good sign.

Even though the relationship didn’t work out, they are still able to refer to each other in friendly ways and show each other a lot of respect. It isn’t always easy to have a friendship with someone you once dated, but these two have mastered the idea completely.

2 Lady Gaga

There have been rumors of a feud between Lana Del Rey and Lady Gaga, but these two popular singers don't have any bad blood between them. This picture is proof that these two are able to hang out without any drama.

Lady Gaga and Lana Del Rey perform very different styles of music, yet for some reason, the two are often compared. Their voices are very different as well, so it is strange that so many people insist on pitting these two women against each other.

1 Demi Lovato

Demi Lovato and Lana Del Rey have a great friendship. They posed together for this adorable selfie. Demi Lovato rose to fame on the Disney Channel, alongside Selena Gomez and Miley Cyrus. Her show was called Sonny with a Chance and she also starred in the Camp Rock movie franchise with the Jonas Brothers.

Lana Del Rey and Demi Lovato have a few things in common when it comes to the use of illegal substances in their past and they have both released songs about the subject.
