]The Weeknd has become one of the most recognized faces in the music industry. However, initially, the star dropped out of high school and ran away from his house. Later, he worked with producer Jeremy Rose and released music on YouTube that gained recognition from notable figures in the music industry, including Drake. Over the years, his albums have sold millions, and he has collaborated with some of the biggest names in Hollywood. Influenced by legends such as Prince and Michael Jackson, the artist has amassed $300 million that he splashes on various luxuries in life. He has an envious car collection, a real estate portfolio, and a notable watch collection that features unconventional and unique watches.

From a custom watch that represents the land of Wakanda to owning a Datejust, The Weeknd has a beautiful collection of selective watches.

Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Concept Black Panther Flying Tourbillon- $162,000

A fan of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, the star contributed to the movies by collaborating with Kendrick Lamar for the soundtrack of Black Panther in 2018. In March 2021, Audemars Piguet announced its collaboration with Marvel by releasing the Royal Oak Concept Black Panther Flying Tourbillon. According to Complex, The Weeknd quickly got his hands on the timepiece and first showed it off on Instagram to his social media followers. The watch has a price tag of $162,000 and is limited to only 250 units. It has a two-tone design in the shade of black and purple that represents the color aesthetics of the movie Black Panther. It is the first product launched since the AP x Marvel collaboration announcement.

Rolex Cellini 'King Midas' - $17,000

During his After Hours-era, the singer exuded the energy of a sleek supervillain for the promotional shoots. Wearing a signature red jacket and glasses, he looked like an antagonist from a late-70s movie. Fans first caught a glimpse of the Rolex Cellini ‘King Midas’ owned by the artist when the promotional photos for the album cover and tour were released for After Hours. His watch is one of the 1000 pieces designed by Gérald Genta, who is known for creating iconic watches, including the Cartier Pasha, Patek Philippe Nautilus, and Audemars Piguet Royal Oak. The King Midas owned by The Weekend has a black bracelet and a black Midas dial. It was also the timepiece worn by Christopher Lee, the Bond villain in The Man With The Golden Gun.

Rolex Onyx Datejust - $35,000

The Weeknd created The Character to go along with the release of his album After Hours, a man who is captivated by superficial beauty and condemned to having procedures on his face. He attended several award shows and gave performances with a swollen and bandaged face. An important part of his outfit was also wearing the Rolex Onyx Datejust that matched his red jacket look. He was sporting the same watch every time he was in The Character at the Video Music Awards, Saturday Night Live, and the American Music Awards. The rare vintage Onyx dial is one of his favorites in the collection, and he was also wearing it during the rehearsal of his Super Bowl Halftime performance in 2021. As mentioned by GQ, it also represents the Hollywood glamour era from the 1980s, and the watch was mentioned 26 times by Christian Bale’s character of Patrick Bateman in American Psycho.

Rolex Day-Date President 40 - $45,000

Another Rolex in the collection owned by the star is the Rolex Day-Date President 40 MM with a champagne dial. The artist had publicly worn the timepiece doing promotional shoots for his single Blinding Lights. The Day-Date President watch has become a popular choice for politicians and diplomats, and celebrities like Lebron James, Drake, and Justin Bieber. The timepiece was first released in 1956 and was the first watch to showcase the day in the full form on the dial, as noted by Rolex. He also owns the timepiece with a champagne dial made from 18-carat yellow gold. It has Rolex’s signature president bracelet and a fluted bezel.

Vacheron Constantin ‘Overseas Automatic’ - $59,000

Known to celebrate everything in style, The Weeknd’s 32nd birthday celebration was no different. In February 2022, he threw a big birthday bash for his close friends in Las Vegas, and Drake shared a series of snaps from the celebration on his Instagram account, as stated by People. The newest addition to the watch collection was a Vacheron Constantin Overseas Automatic shared via pictures. It is the watchmaker's simplest and most coveted watch, and the singer owns the timepiece in a custom black leather strap, 18-carat rose gold, and a blue bezel.

The Weeknd has given subtle glimpses into his watch collection over the years consisting of several high-end timepieces that are rare and expensive today. Through his promotional shoots for the albums, fans may get a closer look into his styling and unconventional watch choices that exude a villain's power. The artist is currently headlining the highly-anticipated Coachella Music Festival.
