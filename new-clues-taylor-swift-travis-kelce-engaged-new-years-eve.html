There were various reports leading up to the holidays that Travis Kelce was going to pop the question to Taylor Swift. But alas, both Christmas and New Year’s came and went without the new couple sharing any engagement news, leading many to believe the reports were wrong.

However, there’s resurfaced speculation that Travis and Taylor did get engaged on NYE thanks to some very telling clues.

Taylor And Travis Reportedly Got Engaged Within Months Of Dating

As per Daily Mail, sources claim that while the couple got engaged in secret, they want to wait until after the Superbowl as well as Taylor’s Eras tour to announce the news.

The anonymous creator behind the Deux Moi podcast – which is known for breaking celebrity scandals both in the podcast episodes as well as on its Instagram account of the same name – alleges a source confirmed the pair are engaged.

“Taylor and Travis got engaged 12/31,” Deux Moi claimed. “They did it just the two of them alone before that party they were photographed at.”

Deux Moi was referring to a viral video from New Year’s Eve that caught the couple in a sweet embrace, with some online lip readers claiming they could see the couple exchanging “I love you.”

Why Taylor And Travis Won’t Reveal Their Alleged Engagement

While the podcast host insisted their information was correct, they said Travis and Taylor likely won’t confirm the news for a while, explaining, “They don't want to overshadow that. It's too soon.”

While they may be keeping an engagement hidden, Travis and Taylor haven’t been shy to express their affection for each other, with footage of the Chiefs star sending the singer a heart sign after scoring a touchdown going viral last week.

Taylor Swift has been happily dating Travis Kelce for the last several weeks, but before him she had plenty of older boyfriends.

Though Taylor seems head-over-heels for Travis, there have been reports she has reservations about their romance, as she’s not sure if it’ll last in the long-term. Moreover, she’s had other big things on her mind – and not just her ongoing Eras tour, which helped her achieve billionaire status last year. Taylor has been dealing with another scary stalker situation as of recently.

Police received reports of a disorderly man trying to enter Taylor’s Tribeca apartment last week. “Upon arrival, [police] were informed that the individual attempted to open a door to a building at the location,” a police representative said in a statement.

"I first saw him around 1 pm, he went up to Taylor's door," a witness told Page Six . "I'm not sure if he knocked or rang the doorbell... He didn't resist. There was no yelling or anything."

Though the man (whose name hasn’t been released publicly) was arrested, he was released within hours and showed up to the singer’s house again.

The stalker incident happened after Travis’ brother Jason Kelce admitted he sees one red flag in Travis and Taylor’s romance – safety.

"It's certainly been weird, the level that it is now," Jason, who’s also an NFL star, told NBCSports. "On one hand, I'm happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he's excited about, that he is genuine about. But there's another end of it where it's like, ‘Man, this is a lot.'"

Taylor hasn’t released a statement on her most recent stalker situation.
