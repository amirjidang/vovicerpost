Vehicles have become more than a part of the lifestyle today in Hollywood. They have become a status symbol, and people keep a close eye on what their favorite celebrities are driving. Celebrities with multi-million dollar fortunes spend thousands of dollars buying the most sought-after cars. While some prefer to own vintage cars in their collection, others buy the most high-tech and customized cars they can get their hands on. As celebs try to expand their collection with luxury cars, Lamborghini has become a must in the car collection. From models and musicians to athletes and actors, every celebrity has a Lamborghini in the garage.

Celebrities have paid extra to customize their luxury Italian supercars with the best accessories and equipment. Some make changes to the interior while others change the outlook of their cars. Let’s take a look at the nicest and most expensive Lamborghinis owned by celebrities.

Chris Brown

Chris Brown has a Goku tattoo and necklace to commemorate Dragon Ball Z, and he completed the set after customizing his Lamborghini Aventador Superveloce with a Dragon Ball Z theme. The Dragon Ball Z hero is painted on the hood with a splash of crimson red, as stated by Auto Evolution. The customized SV cost him a whopping $400,000.

Kanye West

Kanye West is known to make a lot of quirky decisions. When he purchased the Lamborghini Urus in 2019, the rapper decided to modify it himself. The customized Urus has a cream-colored matte wrap with monoblock wheels. The interior is splashed in bright blue leather with grey accents. The starting price of the Urus is $200,000. However, the customized SUV cost a couple thousand more.

Justin Bieber

Justin Bieber purchased his Lamborghini Aventador in 2012 when he was just 18 years old. One of his favorites, the Aventador, had a base price of $119,000 when the singer purchased it. Bieber has wrapped the car many times in different shades. The car was purchased in grey color before it was changed to red. He later painted it to mint blue, and his friend spray-painted the words Cash Only on the door. The car has now regained its original shade of grey.

Jay Leno

Stand-up comedian and late-night talk show host Jay Leno is obsessed with cars. An avid car collector, he is the host of a television series Jay Leno’s Garage. He once showed off an early production Lamborghini from his collection, a 1967 Lamborghini Miura P400. It was the first vehicle called a supercar which became a competitive model in the industry. One of the most beautiful cars in his collection, the Miura P400, is $3.5 million.

50 Cent

One of 50 Cent’s favorite cars in his collection is a Lamborghini Murcielago that he purchased in 2005. The car initially came in the shade of charcoal grey. However, he gave a custom paint job and gave it a blue hue with gold accents. According to Cheat Sheet, the supercar cost him $510,000.

Kim Kardashian

Another Lamborghini Urus on the list is owned by Kanye West’s wife, Kim Kardashian. She wrapped her Urus in SKIMS white cozy fabric to promote her shapewear line. The material covers it in the exterior, seats, rims, and steering wheel. The luxury vehicle cost the reality star a whopping $200,000.

Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner is the youngest in the Kardashian-Jenner clan. However, she has the biggest car collection of them all. While the bright orange color scheme of the Lamborghini Urus might look like an aftermarket wrap, it is a part of Lamborghini’s Pearl Capsule Customization options, as noted by Hot Cars. The limited-edition model of the Pearl Capsule collection has a starting price of $220,000.

Sean Combs

Sean Combs, better known as Diddy, is a Grammy-winning rapper and record producer. With a net worth of over $885 million, Combs has a long list of vehicles in his collection. One of the cars is a Lamborghini Gallardo Spyder that he purchased in 2008. Costing $220,000, the Gallardo Spyder was the best-selling model Lamborghini had ever manufactured and sold.

Offset

Offset celebrated his 29th birthday in style when he was given a surprise gift, a gold Lamborghini Aventador SVJ from his partner Cardi B, as reported by People. With only 63 Aventador SVJs available globally, the model is one of the rarest in the market. Offset was excited and over the moon to receive a $700,000 car as a special birthday gift.

Nicki Minaj

Nicki Minaj loves pink. She even has three studio albums with the name pink in them: Pink Friday, Pink Friday: Roman Reloaded, and The Pinkprint. In 2013, the rapper showed off her bubblegum pink Lamborghini Aventador on Instagram with a customized interior and exterior. The top of the line model cost Minaj $400,000.

Other notable celebrities who also own stunning Lamborghinis include Khloé Kardashian, Pierce Brosnan, and Travis Scott. Paying thousands of dollars, stars love to buy anything they desire, and Lamborghinis are famously irresistible. With a trend to custom-build cars to the heart’s desire, more celebrities will be seen using their creativity to show off their unique Lamborghinis in style.
