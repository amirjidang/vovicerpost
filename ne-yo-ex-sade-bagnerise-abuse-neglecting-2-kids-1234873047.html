Ne-Yo's Ex Sade Bagnerise Accuses Singer Of Abuse And Neglecting Kids

August 26, 2024
post

Sade Bagnerise, the mother of two of Ne’Yo’s children, has accused the R&B singer of physically assaulting her and neglecting their sons, Braiden and Brixton Smith. She has also accused the “Because of You” crooner of engaging in drugs and alcohol around their children.

In an Instagram Live video obtained by LiveBitez on April 25, Bagnerise could be seen and heard having a heated argument with the 44-year-old about their past and current issues — all while their young boys slept.

At one point in the exchange, she even called Ne-Yo “Diddy Jr.” for having women over while their children are present and also claimed that he had “freak-offs.” She even went as far as accusing the star of “body slamming” her. However, she didn’t make it clear if the alleged assault had just happened or was from the past.

“Tell them about the freak-off, Diddy Jr.,” she can be heard saying when the video begins. “You know what you did. You body-slammed me on the floor. Tell them the real you and why we’re here today?” She also continued to question why he “just can’t be at home with his kids.”

“You f**king like to have ho*s, drugs and weed, alcohol, mushrooms, and prostitutes in the house while your kids are here,” she said as Ne-Yo laughed her off. “Everyone has asked him to stop, but he will not listen to nobody. This is what’s going on. This is the tea… I even came over here to help him with these f**king kids because he won’t watch these kids.”

The Arkansas native can also be heard threatening to call the police on Bagnerise, who he said was disturbing the peace and refusing to leave his home.

Last year, Bagnerise publicly dished about her tumultuous co-parenting relationship with Ne-Yo, who she claims has a sex addiction.

“I keep on hearing and seeing all different sides in regards to his behavior & I’ve noticed TWO THINGS,” she wrote last June on her Instagram Story about the Grammy winner.

“Everyone including his mother, associates, myself, and Crystal have all individually said and agree that NONE OF US can recognize this person he has become,” she wrote. “The company that he keeps, which is the primary reason for his demise and DIVORCE (before Crystal ever knew there was a me or kids) is straying him away from the gentle loving man he truly is at heart.”

“They are encouraging his erratic and embarrassing behavior and [sex] addiction. This is like smoking around a junkie,” she continued. “They encourage the alcohol and drugs and outrageous and risky promiscuous lifestyle that may ultimately expose him to diseases he can’t ever get rid of. Unfortunately, he is in the company of users, groupies and bad influences who say yes to everything he says or does.

Earlier this year, Ne-Yo was spotted parading around Jamaica with two women. In footage posted to a woman named Arielle Hill‘s Instagram, the “So Sick” crooner could be seen having the time of his life in a perceived throuple.

One clip showed the trio blowing kisses to the camera as both ladies cuddle up to 44-year-old. Another post showed both ladies posing on a jet ski and standing on the back of his four-wheeler.
