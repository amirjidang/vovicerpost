Professional Career of Sheryl Underwood

After graduating from college, Underwood served two years in the United States Air Force Reserves. Later, in 1989, she became the first female finalist in the Miller Lite Comedy Search. She won the BET “Funniest Female Comedian on Comic View” award in 1994 and the Platinum Mic Viewers’ Choice Award at the BET Comedy Awards in 2005.

Following her success in stand-up comedy, Underwood landed several minor roles in films, including Bad Mouth Bessie in 1998’s I Got the Hook-Up and Catfish Rita in 2005’s Beauty Shop. Underwood was the presenter of BET’s Comic View and the executive producer and host of Holla (September 2002 – September 2003).
