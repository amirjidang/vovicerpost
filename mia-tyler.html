  • 2000: Tyler appeared in the music video for Aerosmith’s song “I Don’t Want to Miss a Thing,” directed by Michael Bay.
  • 2001: Tyler designed a plus-size clothing line for the clothing store Torrid and became the face of the company.

Early Life and Childhood

Mia came into this world on December 22 1978, in Lebanon as the daughter of Steven Tyler and Cyrinda Foxe. Like her father, Steven, her mother, Cyrinda, is an actress famous for her role in “Andy Warhol’s Bad”. Sadly, Cyrinda took her last breath on September 7 2002, due to an inoperable brain tumour.

Mia has a half-sister named Liv Tyler, who has also established a successful career in the entertainment industry and is best known for her role in the “Lord of the Rings” film trilogy. Unfortunately, Mia’s parents separated in 1987, and her mother shifted to New York City in 1990, leaving Mia with her father.

Professional Life

Mia first appeared on television at 17 when she played as VJ in the MTV show “House of Style”. Due to her outstanding skills and beauty, she caught the attention of a modelling agency. She then signed her first modelling contract with Wilhelmina Models, which helped her develop her career to another level. Mia has appeared in several magazines throughout the years, including “Seventeen”, “Moxie Girl”, and “Teen People”. Moreover, she has also worked as a model for brands such as H&M, Lane Bryant, and Pennington. In addition to this, she opened her own Revolution 1228 clothing line in 2009.

2005, she appeared in the VH1 reality show “Celebrity Fit Club”. Later, she was invited to appear in another VH1 show, “Really Rich Real Estate”, in 2006. In March 2009, she appeared as one of the judges in the reality show “Pretty Wicked”, which aired on the Oxygen Network. She also got to play in an episode of the 13th season of “Hell’s Kitchen”. Apart from this, Mia has appeared in several movies and TV series, including “Rush Hour 3”, “People Are Dead”, and “A Little Bit of Lipstick”.

Personal Life

After dating for two years, Brian proposed to Mia for marriage, but they separated before the wedding. A few years later, Mia started dating Dan Halen, and she shares a son, Axton Joseph, who was born on May 10 2017.
