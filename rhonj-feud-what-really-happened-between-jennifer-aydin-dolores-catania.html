When it comes to the Real Housewives of New Jersey you're bound to get heaps of drama, and we mean table flipping, glass throwing, old school family dirty laundry drama.

Well, it seems as if Jersey's tradition of keeping the drama going hasn't died down yet, especially when it comes to Teresa and Margaret's ongoing feud. Now, it seems as if there's another duo at odds, and it's the last two cast members you'd expect.

Despite being part of the iconic RHONJ trio, Dolores Catania and Jennifer Aydin are no longer friends after going head-to-head during the most recent season 12 reunion. While the two had been besties months before, fans are confused over what exactly happened between the two and just why they aren't speaking anymore.

The 'RHONJ' Trio Might Be Done For Good

The Real Housewives of New Jersey cast has undergone quite a few changes since its debut back in 2009.

While Teresa Giudice remains the only original standing housewife on the series and one of the last few of the entire Bravo franchise, the show has kept the same cast for 5 seasons now.

When Dolores Catania and Jennifer Aydin joined the cast years back, a new RHONJ group was formed with Teresa Giudice, and fans immediately became obsessed with the iconic trio.

Dolo, Jen, and Tre — as fans refer to them, always had each other's backs, proving that they were a trio not to be reckoned with. Well, it seems as if the three musketeers are no more after Dolores and Jennifer had a falling out post-season 12 filming.

The two came head to head at the reunion, shocking fans who adored the two's friendship. So, what went wrong? We still don't really know.

Dolores And Jennifer Are Now At Odds

During the first part of the season 12 reunion, Dolores and Jennifer went at it, revealing to Andy Cohen and viewers that the two are no longer friendly.

While the details are fuzzy, considering the two apparently began their feud off-camera, it seems as if it all began after Jennifer felt Dolores didn't have her back this season. It all came down to Dolores telling Jennifer not to cry after Margaret Joseph's brought Jennifer and Bill's marital scruple to the surface.

Jennifer felt as if Dolores didn't support her at that moment, which snowballed into the two no longer being on speaking terms.

'I'm so tired of your backhanded compliments," Jennifer told Dolores during the reunion. Aydin went on to recall a conversation the two had off-camera post-season, however, Dolores called out Jen for reportedly lying about what they had really spoken about.

Dolores Will "Never Be Friends" With Jennifer Again

Things escalated rather quickly during the reunion feud! Dolores went as far as to claim that she will never be friends with Jennifer Aydin again and repeatedly called her "disgusting," while filming the reunion.

Whether it's about loyalty or something fans aren't aware of just yet, it's safe to say that viewers are quite sad and confused over Jen and Dolo's fallout, considering how close the two had been before.

Teresa Giudice & Dolores Catania Are Still Friends

In addition to Dolores being at odds with Jennifer, rumors began swirling that Dolores and Teresa were also not on the best of terms. It was revealed that Teresa did not invite Dolores to her engagement party, which left many fans stunned.

“I would be lying to say it didn’t [hurt] on some level,” Dolores exclusively told Page Six. “Not being invited somewhere shouldn’t hurt me, OK? So I’m going to remind myself of that when these things happen. I am human, but I shake it off, and I’m, like, OK," the RHONJ star said.

Teresa explained herself at the reunion, sharing that they had invited couples they are close to only, and considering Dolores had just broken up with her ex, David at the time, she wasn't included.

Catania understood Tre's reasoning and made it clear that there is no bad blood between the two. Both Teresa and Dolores have been friends for well over 20 years, so fans were happy to hear that they are still on good terms and remain friendly to this day.
