Julia Chavez Rodriguez is a human rights lawyer from the United States and the head of the Strategic Policy Division of the White House. She was elected State Director for Kamala Harris, who was the Senator, in the year 2016.

She has also been consulting on Kamala Harris’ presidential bid for 2020. She is the senior strategist for Latino outreach for Joe Biden’s 2020 presidential campaign from 2020 onwards. She will also act as the head of the Office of Intergovernmental Relations of the White House after Joe Biden exits the office on 20 January 2021.
