The Jonas Brothers And Their Wives: Who Has The Highest Net Worth In 2021?

August 19, 2024
post

The Jonas Brothers broke up in 2013, moved on with their lives and then got back together in 2019 and all seemed right with the world. When they returned all three brothers were married and living their lives. Kevin Jonas was the first to get married. He married Danielle Deleasa in 2009. They have two daughters together- Alena and Valentina. Danielle was not famous when they first met but has since established a name for herself amongst the fandom.

Joe Jonas maried Game of Thrones actress, Sophie Turner in 2019. They had two ceremonies. In July 2020, Joe and Sophie welcomed their first child, a daughter. No official name was released, but rumors have said it's either Will or Hannah. And Nick Jonas married actress Priyanka Jonas in 2018, after only 6 months of dating. They also had numerous ceremonies, celebrating their Hindu and Christian religions. The couple does not have any kids yet.

So, amongst these six, who has the highest net worth this year?

7 Danielle Jonas - $5 Million

It's no surprise that out of everyone in the Jonas clan, Danielle has the lowest net worth. Despite being married to a Jonas Brother, she is not technically famous, though she has established a great career and life for herself. Before she married Kevin, Danielle worked as a hairdresser, which earned her a reasonable income but nothing like it is today. Just by herself, Danielle Jonas' net worth in 2021, is estimated to be around $5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

From 2012 to 2013, she and Kevin appeared on the reality show, Married to Jonas, which chronicled their newly-wed life and family life, which they probably still earn residuals from. In 2018, the 35-year-old founded Danielle Jonas Co., a jewelry company based around birthstones. The company is pretty successful and earns her a lot of income. She is set to release a children's book, "There's A Rock Concert In My Bedroom", in March, 2022, which will only increase her net worth.

6 Sophie Turner - $8 Million

Despite Sophie Turner being the Queen of the North, she is not the richest out of the six. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Turner is estimated to have a net worth of $8 million in 2021. There's no doubt Game of Thrones contributed the most to her income, which she was on from 2011 to 2019. The Emmy-nominated actress has also starred in the X-Men film series, Dark Phoenix, Barely Lethal and more.

Turner has also been featured in ads for Louis Vuitton. The 25-year-old actress is currently starring in The Prince and The Staircase, which will both add to her net worth. Being the youngest of the group, it's not a surprise that her net worth isn't as high as the others. She will only get more popular as time goes on and, therefore, her net worth will increase. Her and Joe also just bought a $14 million mansion in California.

5 Priyanka Chopra Jonas - $30 Million

Priyanka Chopra Jonas is one of the highest-paid Indian actresses, according to Forbes. Websites estimate her net worth to be around $30 to $45 million, with her earning at least $10 million a year. Being the oldest of the group, she has been in the entertainment business the longest and has earned a massive net worth. Her career started in 2002, but she rose to prominence in in 2005 when she starred in six films, including Yakeen and Barsaat. The 39-year-old started out in Bollywood films before moving to bigger Hollywood roles, such as Baywatch, Quantico, Isn't It Romantic? and more.

Along with being an actress, she is also a producer, singer, model and writer. Chopra Jonas released her memoir, Unfinished, in February 2021. The actress is also an endorser for many companies, such as Pepsi, Nikon, Pantene, Garnier, Lyf Mobile and more, which account for half of her income. She has also invested in a lot of companies, including Bumble, and owns a total of nine properties. Her and Nick recently bought a $20 million mansion.

4 Kevin Jonas - $40 Million

A significant jump happens between the wives and the brothers. Kevin Jonas' net worth is estimated to be around $40 to $50 million, according to multiple websites. When the Jonas Brothers broke up, Kevin went back to living a normal life. He dabbled in real estate for a bit, founding a real estate development, construction company called JonasWerner and made a living doing that. He also starred in Married To Jonas and is the CEO of The Blu Market Company. Sadly, they filed for Chapter 11 Bankruptcy in 2018.

The 33-year-old also appeared on a season of The Celebrity Apprentice and developed a food app called Yood. The guitarist is also a partner of the video-sharing app, We Heart It. Along with all of these adventures, Kevin earns the most of his earnings from The Jonas Brothers. Between albums sales, touring, merchandise and more, the band has earned him the most money.

3 Joe Jonas - $40 Million

Joe Jonas also has an estimated net worth of $40 to $50 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Along with earning money from the band, the singer has earned an impressive living for himself. In 2011, he released his first solo album, Fastlife, and toured with Britney Spears on her European tour. When the Jonas Brothers went on hiatus, the 32-year-old formed the successful group, DNCE, in which he was the lead singer. Their single, "Cake By The Ocean," peaked at number 9 on the US Billboard Hot 100. The band released two EPs and a full-length album. They opened for Selena Gomez and Bruno Mars on their tours.

In 2018, Joe also joined The Voice Australia as a coach, after being a mentor on the American version. Jonas is also dabbling in acting and joined the movie, "Devotion," which is set to premiere next year. His net worth continues to increase as he spends time in the band and will continue to act and release new music.

2 Nick Jonas - $50 Million

Despite all of the brothers being in the band together, Nick has a bit of a higher net worth than the other two. Celebrity Net Worth estimates his to be around $50 million. Him and Chopra have about an estimated combined net worth of $70 million, making them the richest Jonas couple. Besides his time with the Jonas Brothers, Nick has put out four solo albums, including Spaceman, which he released this year. He has been on solo tours as well as opening acts for Bruno Mars and Maroon 5 and a tour with Demi Lovato.

Besides singing Nick has the most acting credits out of the brothers including Kingdom, Jumanji and its sequel, Chaos Walking, Ugly Dolls, Midway and more. He also produces the streaming show, Dash and Lily. The 29-year-old was a coach on The Voice in season 18 and 20 and has dabbled in theater. Nick has also released a shoe, clothing and fragrance brand, JV x NJ, and makes a lot of his money from the tequila he created called Villa One. As long as he keeps acting and singing, the "This is Heaven" singer's net worth will continue to increase.

1 Total Band Net Worth - $150 Million

The Jonas Brothers hit the Forbes' top twenty most celebrity earnings in 2020 with $68.5 million. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the band has a total combined net worth of $150 million in 2021. Despite the pandemic happening, the brothers had just wrapped up their comeback tour, The Happiness Begins tour, which earned them a ton of money. During 2020, the band released singles and merchandise that kept their income flowing.

They continue to get residuals from Camp Rock movies and their past albums. Now, the Jonas Brothers are currently on their second tour since they reunited, the Remember This tour. Despite it only being a U.S. tour for now, due to COVID-19 restrictions, Kevin, Joe and Nick's net worth as a band are sure to increase significantly by the end of the year.
