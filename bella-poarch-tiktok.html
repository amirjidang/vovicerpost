Bella Poarch is a TikTok star who has recently taken the internet by storm, earning 73.7 million followers on the app. She has set records for content creation including having the most viewed video of the year on TikTok. She has showcased her diverse talents and ability to break into the mainstream by launching a career in music and landing a spot on the billboard charts. Through her ability to cross platform, she has also earned over 12.3 million followers on Instagram.

But, Bella Poarch has come to her success through overcoming a difficult life with hard work and experience that sets her apart from other social stars in her demographic. Poarch was raised in the Philippines, she enlisted in the United States Navy, and was bullied, which later served as inspiration throughout her music career. But through her perseverance, Bella Poarch has become one of the most popular stars on the tikTok platform who provides a welcome space for fans to feel comfortable just being themselves.

Childhood

Bella Poarch grew up in the Philippines with her grandmother.  She was adopted at a young age and didn't move to the United States until she was in her teens. She reflected on her life in the Philippines as tough, recalling traumatic moments with her stepfather on the H3H3 podcast. 

Time in the Navy

Poarch is also one of the few social stars to also have served in the Navy. She enlisted back in 2015 and served three years in several locations. She has spoken favorably of her experience on Instagram and on podcasts. 

TikTok

Bella Poarch was a TikTok hit early on. In August when she posted a simple video of her lip synching, it earned a spot of the ForYou page and went viral. She gained 36.5 million likes and over 140 million views. 

She has also appeared in uniform on several of her TikTok videos. Her viral moment came when she shared a video featuring the track "M to the B".

Viral Friendships

Bella Poarch is also known for keeping herself in good company. She has posted pictures on Instagram with several other celebrities including James Charles.

She is also known for having appeared as a guest on the popular H3H3 Podcast alongside Hila and Ethan Klein. She has also appeared in a YouTube series, 100 Thieves. In addition, she was a gust on the Bretman Rock YouTube channel.

Career in Music

After making a variety of celebrity friends, she incorporated them and featured them in her music career. Leveraging her social media following, Bella Poarch made the decision to pursue another of her passions, music. She released the song "Build a B*tch" which earned her not only a spot on the Billboard Top 100 charts, but also with nearly 200 million views on YouTube. The video of the single also featured a laundry list of whose-who influencers ranging from Larray and Mia Khalifa to Valkyrae and ZHC. Controversy surrounded the release of the song as many news outlets noticed the similarities of the songs melody to another track originally written by Mozart. 

Poarch is continuing to release music and is set to go on tour with That Suburban in September of 2020. The groups will tour together throughout Chicago, California, and other major US cities. She is also expected to release a full length album sometime in the near future.

The Future For Bella

With Tiktok, virality, and music careers already secured under her belt, the next step for Bella Poarch is open. After a popular appearance on the h3h3 podcast and a willingness to discuss her life openly, maybe she will choose the path of fellow TikTok star Addison Rae who started her own Spotify-exclusive podcast.

A makeup line may also be inevitable for the star as she frequently shared her friendship with James Charles. She has also shared pictures with the viral Paca the Alpaca. Poarch has also explored the world of 3d art and animation. She has posted 3D creations including fictional versions of herself located within the metaverse. The cover art for her first official single included both fantasy elements with dark futuristic elements which might also open up the potential for future NFT releases. 

Bella Poarch has showcased her unique ability to tap into the hearts and minds of an entire generation of viewers. Her strength revolves around her honesty and ability to reflect on her life experiences in a way that helps her audiences feel less alone. Poarch has shown that despite exterior circumstances, someone who is willing to work hard, think innovatively, and produce content has the capacity to become one of the most viewed stars on social media platforms.
